The **Personal Finance Dashboard** is a risk assessment interface designed to help users understand the risk profile of various asset classes.

### 3. Navigation
The home screen presents 4 major asset classes:
1.  **Savings Accounts** (Low Risk)
2.  **Government Bonds** (Low-Medium Risk)
3.  **Index Funds** (Medium-High Risk)
4.  **Cryptocurrency** (Extreme Risk)

Click on any card to view detailed historical performance and risk metrics for that asset class.
